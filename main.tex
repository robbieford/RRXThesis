\begin{abstract}
Digital communications continues to be an interesting field of study as new technologies appear and old methodologies get revisited or renovated. The goal of this research was to look into the old digital communication scheme of Bell 202 and find a way to demodulate the signal in software with results similar to those of hardware demodulators. The research shows that through using Sivan Toledoâ€™s JavaAX25 software package, new demodulation algorithms can be implemented that decode Bell 202 AX.25 encoded packets that the existing software tool could not.
